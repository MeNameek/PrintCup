About This Project

This Mini Club World Cup Trophy is a 3D printable collectible designed for fans and football lovers. The model was sourced online and optimized to print perfectly at 7.8 cm tall — a size that fits anywhere from a desktop to a shelf.

We created this campaign to let fans celebrate the excitement of the Club World Cup, even if they can’t get their hands on the real thing.

It’s made using eco-friendly PLA plastic and prints on most standard 3D printers. Feel free to paint it or customize it as you like!
